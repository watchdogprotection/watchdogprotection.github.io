3.2 Security Measures

4. Telegram Integration

Watchdog uses Telegram's Bot API to send you notifications. When you register:

  • We only access your Telegram Chat ID (a number, not your username or phone)
  • We detect your language preference from Telegram settings
  • We cannot read your messages or access your contacts
  • You can unlink your device anytime using /unregister command

Telegram's Privacy Policy: https://telegram.org/privacy

5. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

Right How to Exercise It
Access Your Data Send /status command to our Telegram bot
